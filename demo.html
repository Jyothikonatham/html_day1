In HTML, the paragraph tag is used to define a paragraph of text. It's a block-level element that typically starts on a new line and is visually separated from other paragraphs by blank lines or white space.

Formatting Tags

The bold tag is used to make text bold for styling purposes.

The strong tag is used to define text with strong importance.

In HTML, the i tag is used to display text in an italic font style, typically for words or phrases that should be set apart or emphasized.

In HTML , the u tag is used to underline text.

The small HTML tag is used to display text in a smaller font size, often for side comments, copyright notices, or similar secondary information.

In HTML, the emphasis tag em is used to highlight or stress a specific part of text, typically displayed as italics.

The big tag in HTML 4 was used to make the enclosed text one font size larger than the surrounding text
